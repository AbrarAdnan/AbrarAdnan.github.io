    What is Brutown Connect?

    It follows a loose principle from “Mens sana in corpore sano” a healthy mind in a healthy body! Brutown Connect aspires to form a hub for the healthy mind to work in an ideal space comprising of thinkers and doers. An area that can accommodate 30 individuals comprised of entrepreneurs, freelancers, startups!
